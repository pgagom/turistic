01 — Description

What is
Barranco?

District south of Lima · Near the Pacific

Barranco is the most bohemian and romantic neighborhood in Lima. Its streets are colorful and full of art, with old houses with colorful facades and murals on the walls.

🎨 Street art 🌊 Next to the Pacific 🏛️ Old architecture ☀️ Mild weather 🌙 Cool at night

02 — Places & Activities

What to
visit

The must-sees of the district

🌉 Bridge of Sighs — the most iconic; make a wish when you cross it.
🌅 The Boardwalk — a walk by the sea, perfect to watch the sunset.
🖼️ MAC Museum — modern paintings and sculptures.
⛪ Main Square — old church, trees and a calm atmosphere.
🏖️ Agua Dulce Beach — a nearby beach to relax.
🎵 Live music 🛍️ Handicrafts 📸 Urban murals

04 — Gastronomy

Flavors of
Barranco

Traditional food and cafés

🐟 Ceviche — fresh fish with lemon, chili and onion.
🍢 Anticuchos — grilled beef heart with potatoes and corn.
🍮 Suspiro a la Limeña — dessert made with condensed milk and meringue.
☕ Cafés — cozy places with coffee and pastries for everyone.

There are options for all tastes and budgets.
